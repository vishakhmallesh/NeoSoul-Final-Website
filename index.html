Services


NeoSoul is Bengaluru based software solutions company. We help each of our clients create and maintain a digital presence truly worthy of their brand.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

We use the power of GPUs and Deep learning techniques to solve problems that have a higher complexity.

Mobile and Web Apps Development

We like to keep it simple. Our goals are to focus on typography, content and conveying the message that you want to send.

IoT

About

  • 2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The idea for NeoSoul sparked in the year of 2014. Our goal is to develop products in the field of agriculture and hospitality management equipped Artificial Intelligence.

  • August 2016

    An Agency is Born

    NeoSoul Software Solutions Pivate Ltd. took its first officially steps in 2016 with just 3 people working out of a small office.

  • Our First Project

    We built our very first project for restaurants in tier 2 cities. The automation software is a huge success.  

  • Our Next Steps

    We are currently working on building an ecosystem for agriculture, horticulture and livestocks. Apart from this, we are building a deep neural networks for form identification and correction of athletes and yoga practioners.
